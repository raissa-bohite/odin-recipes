Cheesy Pickle Pigs in a Blanket

Description

For these cheesy pickle pigs in a blanket, a cornichon is surrounded by bacon and cheese in
a rolled-up crescent roll, and baked until golden. It's a perfect party appetizer.

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 35 mins
Servings: 16
Yield: 32 pieces Home
